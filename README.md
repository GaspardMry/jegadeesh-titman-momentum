# Jegadeesh-Titman Momentum Strategy

## Overview
Python replication of the seminal Jegadeesh & Titman (1993) momentum strategy,
one of the most robust and documented anomalies in financial markets.

## Concepts Covered
- **Momentum Effect** — stocks that outperformed over the past 3-12 months 
tend to continue outperforming in the short term
- **Formation & Holding Periods** — ranking stocks on past returns, 
holding winners and shorting losers
- **Long/Short Portfolio** — buying top decile, shorting bottom decile
- **Factor Replication** — academic paper implementation in Python
